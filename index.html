From Director's Desk

Walchand College Of Engineering, Sangli.

SOFTA has always been regular in improving the performance of all its members. Regular club service helps students to keep up with the pace of the ever growing competition by enhancing and improvising their personality traits. Club service not only provides a straightforward way to communicate with the senior students but also helps to have benevolence among all the students. I believe it to be a way of making contacts which will help them in the corporate world when they step out of this college. Club service has also made it a practice for the students to have continuous enhancement of their personality along with proper concentration on academics. The technical knowledge shared in these club services adds to the knowledge of the students. Students develop awareness towards the new innovations and happenings around the world which helps them to keep an updated knowledge bank. It has also been actively hosting events like Caliber and Intra-Club Team Event helping the students to enhance their personality and to ensure overall development.

It gives me huge bliss to have an elite club SOFTA in our college. SOFTA has been an active participant in all the major events of college. SOFTA hosts events like IMPULSE which not only provides a platform for students of our college but also across the University to judge their own personality. It has been great to see that IMPULSE is achieving greater heights of success every year. This club has also been involved heartily in Graduation Day and F. E. Admission process. I feel proud to see students of WCE taking efforts towards enhancing their skills by involving in SOFTA. I wish SOFTA to succeed in all the steps it would take to enhance quality and performance of the students of this club.

Dr. G. V. Parishwad
Director, WCE, Sangli
